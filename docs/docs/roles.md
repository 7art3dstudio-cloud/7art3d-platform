# 7Art3D User Roles

## Buyer
- Browse products
- Purchase 3D models
- Leave reviews
- Follow creators

## Designer
- Share projects
- Build a portfolio
- Get community feedback

## Seller
- Upload products
- Sell digital models
- Earn revenue from sales

## Student
- Access free models
- Learn through tutorials
- Join challenges
- Limited uploads and actions in the MVP

## Progression
Student → Designer → Seller
